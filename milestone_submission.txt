Q4. Bonding Curve Validation Report: Provide a bonding curve validation report demonstrating that the bonding curve is functioning as expected.*
The Nanobond bonding curve is functioning as designed and is publicly verifiable through both the contract code and live market activity.

Validation details:
- The bonding curve logic is implemented in `nanobondtoken/src/NanobondExponentialCurve.sol`.
- Pricing is computed by `currentPrice()` using an exponential growth model based on `basePrice`, `growthRateBP`, and `stepSize`.
- `buy()` calculates the current price from circulating supply, mints new NANO, transfers tokens to the buyer, and increases both `internalTotalSupply` and `totalRaisedHbar`.
- `sell()` calculates the current sell value from the same pricing curve, transfers tokens back into the contract, burns them, and returns HBAR to the seller.
- Because price depends on `internalTotalSupply`, every buy and sell changes the effective spot price and market position on the curve.

Observed validation on mainnet:
- Public trading activity is visible at `https://app.nanobond.xyz/trade`.
- Buying and selling on the live market changes price and bonding curve state in real time.
- The dashboard exposes the resulting supply, price, holder distribution, and total volume, confirming that the tokenomics model is active in production rather than simulated.

This confirms the bonding curve is live, responsive to market activity, and behaving according to the exponential pricing model implemented in the contract.
